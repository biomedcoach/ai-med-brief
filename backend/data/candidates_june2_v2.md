**Summary (EN):** At ASCO 2026, China's ADC sector achieved three landmark advances. Third, the target landscape expanded from a handful (e.g., HER2) to a broad portfolio including TROP2, CLDN18.2, B7-H3, and Nectin-4, covering lung, gastric, breast, and urothelial cancers. With a record number of oral presentations, China has vaulted from ADC follower to a core global innovation hub.

**Title (EN):** ASCO 2026: Alpha Isotope Ignition — China's Radiopharma Races for Global Leadership in the Beta-to-Alpha Transition
**摘要 (CN):** 2026年ASCO年会上，放射性配体疗法（RLT）和放射性核素偶联药物（RDC）成为全场焦点。这类"核药"通过靶向分子将放射性核素精准递送至肿瘤细胞，实现"诊疗一体化"——既能定位病灶，又能定点杀伤。诺华在会上展示了其明星产品Pluvicto（¹⁷⁷Lu-PSMA-617）在不同疾病负荷下的疗效数据，进一步验证了靶向放射性疗法的临床价值。更重要的趋势是：全球核药研发正从β核素（如¹⁷⁷Lu）向α核素（如²²⁵Ac）加速切换——α粒子射程更短（仅几层细胞）、能量更高、对周围健康组织的损伤更小，有望将核药疗效推向新高度。中国企业如远大医药、东诚药业等已抢先布局α核素管线，中国丰富的同位素堆产能力和快速成长的CRO/CDMO体系为核药国产化提供了独特优势，ASCO 2026或将成为中国核药从跟跑到领跑的转折点。
**Summary (EN):** Radioligand therapy (RLT) and radionuclide drug conjugates (RDC) dominated ASCO 2026. These "radiopharmaceuticals" use targeting vectors to deliver radioactive isotopes precisely to tumor cells, achieving "theranostics" — simultaneous imaging and therapy. Novartis presented Pluvicto (¹⁷⁷Lu-PSMA-617) efficacy data across varying disease burdens, solidifying the clinical value of targeted radiotherapy. The crucial trend: the global radiopharma pipeline is accelerating its shift from beta-emitters (e.g., ¹⁷⁷Lu) to alpha-emitters (e.g., ²²⁵Ac) — alpha particles have a shorter range (just a few cell layers), higher linear energy transfer, and cause less collateral damage to healthy tissue, potentially elevating radiopharma efficacy to a new level. Chinese enterprises including Grand Pharma and Dongcheng Pharmaceutical have proactively built alpha-isotope pipelines. China's abundant isotope production capacity and rapidly maturing CRO/CDMO ecosystem offer unique advantages for domestic radiopharma, making ASCO 2026 a potential inflection point for China's transition from follower to leader.
