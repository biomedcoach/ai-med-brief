# Baituan AI Launches Health Model Baichuan-M4 with 3.3% Hallucination Rate

> Published May 31, 2026 | Source: 36kr_ai

Baituan AI, led by Wang Xiaochuan, unveiled its new health-focused large language model Baichuan-M4 on May 26, 2026, achieving the lowest hallucination rate in medical queries at 3.3%, outperforming top general models like GPT-5.5. The model excels in HealthBench evaluations, with a strong emphasis on factual accuracy for reliable clinical applications. Baituan AI's strategic shift to healthcare follows a broader trend in China's AI industry, where startups like Baituan are moving from general AI to specialized verticals for sustainable growth. This pivot is underscored by partnerships with leading hospitals, such as achieving 95% accuracy in pediatric AI doctor consultations, highlighting the potential for AI to enhance diagnostic precision and patient care in resource-constrained settings.

## Key Points

- Baituan AI's Baichuan-M4 model demonstrates a groundbreaking 3.3% hallucination rate in medical queries, significantly reducing errors in AI-driven healthcare diagnostics compared to industry standards.
- The strategic pivot by Baituan AI and other 'six small tigers' from general AI to healthcare applications reflects a market reality where specialization is key to achieving commercial viability and reducing reliance on costly infrastructure.
- This shift underscores the growing importance of AI in medical devices and diagnostics, potentially improving patient outcomes and setting a precedent for global AI healthcare innovation.
