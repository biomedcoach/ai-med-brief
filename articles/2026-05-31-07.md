# MiniMax Files A-Share IPO Filing, Reports ARR Growth to Over $300 Million

> Published May 31, 2026 | Source: 36kr_ai

MiniMax, a Shanghai-based leader in AI large language models, submitted an A-share IPO filing on May 29, 2026, with CITIC Securities as the advisor, following its Hong Kong IPO in 2026. The company's stock surged 409% to 840 HKD, valuing it at approximately $227.5 billion USD. Financially, MiniMax disclosed an ARR exceeding $300 million in 2026, with 2025 revenue of $79.038 million, driven by AI-native products and enterprise services, and a gross margin of 25.4%. The company has released models like MiniMax-M3 with enhanced sparse attention mechanisms, improving processing speeds by up to 15.6x. This IPO filing highlights the trend of AI firms securing capital for innovation amid high computational costs and market competition.

## Key Points

- MiniMax filed for A-share IPO on May 29, 2026, with a market capitalization of $227.5 billion USD following stock growth.
- The company's ARR exceeded $300 million in 2026, demonstrating significant revenue expansion and a 100% growth in ARR over the past two months.
- MiniMax-M3 model features improved sparse attention mechanisms, offering faster inference and decoding speeds, which could enhance AI performance across applications.
