# LinuTech Intelligence Launches AI Brain Imaging Systems Reducing Analysis Time to 10 Minutes

> Published May 31, 2026 | Source: leiphone_ai

In a May 2024 interview, Shen Dingang, CEO of Chinese medical AI company LinuTech Intelligence, detailed the launch of two innovative AI systems for MRI brain imaging: the 'MR Brain Resting-State Functional Intelligent Analysis System' and 'MR Brain Fiber Intelligent Analysis System'. These tools, built on years of research, automate complex data processing, with the fiber system completing DTI analysis in under 10 minutes, compared to traditional hours-long workflows. This full-stack AI platform supports comprehensive brain studies, from pediatric development to neurodegenerative diseases, enabling efficient research and clinical applications. The advancements highlight LinuTech's commitment to AI-driven medical technology, potentially improving diagnostic speed and accuracy in neurology without regulatory claims.

## Key Points

- LinuTech Intelligence's new AI systems reduce DTI analysis time to 10 minutes, significantly speeding up brain imaging workflows.
- The full-stack uAI platform integrates brain imaging tools for comprehensive research and clinical support across multiple neurological conditions.
- These innovations advance AI in medical diagnostics by making complex brain imaging more accessible and efficient for healthcare professionals.
