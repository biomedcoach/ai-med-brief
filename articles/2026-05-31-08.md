# China's Tianjin AI Expo Showcases Non-Humanoid Robots and Medical AI Interfaces

> Published May 31, 2026 | Source: 36kr_ai

At the May 28, 2026, Tianjin Intelligent Industry Expo, a variety of AI and robotics innovations were displayed, including China Unicom's AI mechanical arm that completes car door defect detection in 5 minutes, enhancing industrial efficiency and safety. Also featured were brain-computer interfaces from Aoyi Technology, which are being explored for medical rehabilitation to assist patients with movement disorders, potentially improving outcomes in healthcare. This event underscores China's growing investment in AI applications across sectors, addressing real-world challenges and paving the way for broader adoption in both industrial and medical contexts. The expo highlights the practical integration of AI, with companies like Huawei and Sinopec showcasing advancements that could lead to significant cost savings and performance improvements.

## Key Points

- Non-humanoid robots, such as mechanical arms, are used in industrial settings to reduce risks and compress tasks like defect detection to 5 minutes.
- Aoyi Technology's brain-computer interfaces are being developed for medical rehabilitation, aiming to aid patients with neural disorders through neural feedback.
- The Tianjin expo demonstrates China's focus on practical AI applications, emphasizing innovations that enhance efficiency and address challenges in industry and healthcare.
