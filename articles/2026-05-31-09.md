# Alibaba Health's H2O+ AI Platform Launches Exclusive Partnership with BMJ Group

> Published May 31, 2026 | Source: leiphone_ai

On May 13, 2026, Alibaba Health introduced its medical AI platform, H2O+, announcing an exclusive content partnership with BMJ Group, a publisher of 70 international medical journals including The BMJ. This collaboration provides Chinese doctors direct access to global evidence sources for clinical and research queries, addressing barriers like language, access costs, and AI inaccuracies. BMJ's chief digital officer emphasized the innovation in knowledge delivery, while Alibaba Health's CEO highlighted the platform's four-layer evidence-based architecture to minimize 'AI hallucinations.' The deal supports efforts to upgrade H2O+ from an information tool to a trusted 'second brain' for evidence-based medicine, potentially setting industry standards in China and aiding resource-dispersed healthcare settings.

## Key Points

- H2O+ AI gains exclusive access to BMJ Group's 70 medical journals, enabling direct evidence retrieval and translation for Chinese healthcare professionals.
- The partnership addresses structural barriers in accessing global medical research, potentially improving clinical decision-making and research efficiency.
- This collaboration underscores China's push for evidence-based AI standards, aiming to influence global medical technology governance.
